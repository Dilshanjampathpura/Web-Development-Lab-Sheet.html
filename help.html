E-ticket Booking


  1. Select Preferred Option:
    • Choose the option that best suits your preferences in terms of timing, price, and convenience.

  2. Select Seat/Class:
    • For some modes of transport, such as trains and airplanes, you may have the option to select your preferred seat or class.

  3. Provide Passenger Details:
    • Enter the required details for each passenger, including names, ages, and any other relevant information.

  4. Review Booking Details:
    • Review the details of your booking, including the journey details, passenger information, and total price.

  5. Make Payment:
    • Proceed to the payment page and choose your preferred payment method (credit/debit card, net banking, mobile wallet, etc.). Enter your payment details and complete the transaction.

  6. Receive Confirmation:
    • Once the payment is successful, you will receive a booking confirmation along with a booking reference number via email or SMS.

  7. Download or Print Ticket:
    • Depending on the transport provider and the mode of transport, you may have the option to download and print your ticket or receive an e-ticket that you can show on your mobile device.

Online Seat Selection

  1. View Seat Map:
    • Once you select a bus, you'll typically see a seat map showing the layout of the bus and the available seats.

  2. Choose Seats:
    • Click on the seats you want to select. Available seats are usually shown in a different color or marked as available.

  3. Confirm Seat Selection:
    • After selecting your seats, confirm your selection. Some platforms might ask you to confirm the selected seats before proceeding.

  4. Provide Passenger Details:
    • Enter the required details for each passenger, including names and any other relevant information.

  5. Review Booking Details:
    • Review the details of your booking, including the selected seats, journey details, passenger information, and total price.

  6. Make Payment:
    • Proceed to the payment page and choose your preferred payment method. Enter your payment details and complete the transaction.

  7. Receive Confirmation:
    • Once the payment is successful, you will receive a booking confirmation along with a booking reference number via email or SMS

  8. Board the Bus:
    • Arrive at the departure point (bus station or stop) at least a few minutes before the scheduled departure time. Show your ticket or booking confirmation to the bus staff before boarding.
